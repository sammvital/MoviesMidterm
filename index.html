Movies with Sam

Cinematic reviews of my three all time favorite moveis

About

These are my all time favorite movies ... I am in no way a movie critic nor claiming to be. Rom-coms are my favorite as we can tell but shout out to all the actors and actresses in these movies as they are legends. If you have not seen these films I highly reccomend.

The Bodyguard

Summary

This movie comes in at number one because of the comfort! It is there for me when I am sad or feeling down and 100% cheers me up. It is a tear jerker but in a cute rom-com kind of way. This film feautres Whitney Houston (which mean amazing music!!!) Kevin Costner (very dreamy) including a little action - what else do you need?!

Best-selling pop diva Rachel Marron (Whitney Houston) has a stalker whose obsession has risen to the level of disturbing threats. At the urging of her manager (Gary Kemp), Rachel hires former secret service agent Frank Farmer (Kevin Costner) as her bodyguard. Initially resented and treated with disdain for his hard-nosed security procedures, Farmer soon becomes an integral part of Rachel's inner circle. As they spend more time together, client and protector become closer still.

Dirty Dancing

Summary

Coming in at number two - another feel good movie. Another cute rom-com a little bit older but great music as well, including one of the greatest movie songd of all time "(I've had) The time of my Life". Another amazing cast with Jennifer Grey as the main female lead and Patrick Swayze in the 80's (can't get better than that!).

Baby (Jennifer Grey) is one listless summer away from the Peace Corps. Hoping to enjoy her youth while it lasts, she's disappointed when her summer plans deposit her at a sleepy resort in the Catskills with her parents. Her luck turns around, however, when the resort's dance instructor, Johnny (Patrick Swayze), enlists Baby as his new partner, and the two fall in love. Baby's father forbids her from seeing Johnny, but she's determined to help him perform the last big dance of the summer.

Friends with Benefits

Summary

Another amazing movie again a tear jerker buttt it makes up for it with Mila Kunis and Justin Timberlake! Your typcial rom-rom taking place in NYC where freinds began to fall in love actually getting to know each other. Just another feel good movie with lots of laughs!

Jamie (Mila Kunis) is a New York-based executive recruiter who entices Dylan (Justin Timberlake), an art director from Los Angeles, to take a job at the New York office of GQ magazine. Finding that they have much in common, the two become fast friends. Feeling jaded by a number of broken romances, Dylan and Jamie decide that they are ready to quit looking for true love and focus on having fun. However, complications unfold when the two best pals add sex to their relationship.
